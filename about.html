About Us

Asian Allied Freight Management Corporation provides a
wide range of logistical services to meet the ever growing
demand for domestic and international freight forwarding.

We understand the value of our clients’ shipments, that’s
why everything is being handled with care. We provide
ourselves with the level of professionalism and excellence
that our company has to offer.

Our Mission

To establish a professionally managed organization that
is able to provide the best quality freight management
services to meet our clients’ demands and expectations.

Our Vision

Asian Allied Freight Management Corp. will be the leading
global logistics provider and build the road map
to a world class supply chain management
infrastructure.
